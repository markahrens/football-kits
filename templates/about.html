About

I have a particular fondness for football kits. I feel like each one tells a bit of a story about the club. Many clubs have storied histories of colors and/or patters for their kits. When a club deviates from their history, it is always big news.

Then you have sponsors with huge clubs like Arsenal being sponsored by a worldwide airline like Emirates to smaller clubs sponsored by a local company. You can get a sense of the importance of a club based on their sponsor.

While the Wikipedia will often have a spot showing off the design for a club's kit, it doesn't have a great way to see a history of the kit through the years. Because of this, I wanted to develop a site that could be relatively easily maintained by anyone interested in this topic. This site is built with code hosted on Github. Anyone can fork the project to add or edit data for a club using the instructions there. I am excited to see where this can go!

--Mark Ahrens
